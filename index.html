About

Behind the lens

I'm B.M.M, a visual storyteller with over a decade of experience capturing moments that transcend the ordinary. My work spans the majestic landscapes of East Africa to intimate portraits in urban studios across Europe and Asia.

Trained at the Royal College of Art and mentored by masters of documentary photography, I blend technical precision with intuitive composition. Every frame I produce is deliberate — a conversation between light, subject, and the unseen.

My work has appeared in National Geographic, Vogue Africa, and The Guardian, and has been recognized with multiple World Press Photo commendations.
